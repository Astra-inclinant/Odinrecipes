Fish en Papillote

Description

Fish en Papillote is a French method of cooking fish to ensure pleasing flavor and optimal texture. The fish is encased in a packet of parchment paper, along with fresh vegetables, white wine, and butter.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 red onion, julienned
  • 1 zuchinni, julienned
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 (4-ounce) cod fillets
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • 8 springs fresh thyme
  • 4 pats butter
  • 1/4 cup white wine

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. In a bowl mix together the onion, zuchinni,and garlic.Add the oil, season with salt and pepper, toss to combine.
  3. Place each fish fillet on a large square of parchment paper, Arrange vegetables on top.
  4. Layer atop the vegetables the thyme, butter, and splash with white wine.
  5. Fold over fish, and crimp to seal tightly. Arrange on baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes
