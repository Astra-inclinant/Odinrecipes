Banana curry pork loin

Description

Tender pork loin cooked in a cream based curry sauce alongside caramelized banana halves

Ingredients

  • 2 pound pork loin
  • One cup of heavy cream
  • Tbsp of yellow curry powder
  • Kosher salt and fresh ground black pepper
  • 3 ripe bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Trim excess fat from pork loin and season all over with Salt and ground black pepper
  3. Sear pork loin on all sides until golden brown
  4. Rest pork loin for ten minutes and slice into 1" thick rounds
  5. Place pork into a roasting pan in overlapping layers
  6. Add heavy cream to pan and reduce slightly, before adding curry powder
  7. In a new pan add a small amount of oil, slice bananas in half lengthwise
  8. Sear bananas on both sized until caramelized golden-brown
  9. Drape bananas over pork and pour over the curry-cream sauce
  10. Roast for thirty minutes
